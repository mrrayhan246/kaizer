----- ABOUT US

Kaizer Knitwears Ltd started its journey in the year 2004. Today, Kaizer Knitwears Ltd has become one of the top knit-based textile manufacturing powerhouses in Bangladesh, with a fully vertical setup that covers every aspect of production, from knitting and dyeing to sewing, embroidery, and printing recognized by renowned brands such as Bang Bang, Anna, Vegotex, Fox, New Yorker and Concept Club. Kaizer Knitwears Ltd is able to maintain a dynamic supply chain that can handle even the shortest lead times.
